Our Story

Brew Haven was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to create a warm, welcoming space where coffee lovers could enjoy exceptional drinks made with care and expertise.

We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers around the world, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable growing practices. Each batch is roasted in-house to bring out the unique character of every variety.

Beyond great coffee, we're committed to fostering community. Our shop hosts regular events, from cupping sessions to local artist showcases, creating a space where connections are made over the perfect cup.
